Privacy

Privacy-first by default

I aim for minimal data collection and maximal transparency. Any data that an app needs will be explained in plain language inside the app and reflected in its Google Play “Data safety” disclosures.

  • No hidden tracking: if analytics are ever used, they’ll be explicitly disclosed and optional where possible.
  • Least-privilege permissions: apps request only what they genuinely need.
  • Clear controls: export/delete options where relevant, and straightforward settings.

Note: specific privacy terms may vary per app depending on features (e.g., cloud sync). This page will be kept current.
